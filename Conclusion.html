Conclusion

Even though we have learned a great deal about medieval Bosnia thanks to Bosnian tombstones, we have only have only discovered a small piece of the puzzle. Unfortunately, very few items from that age have been preserved. Nonetheless, I hope in that in the future, we will be able learn more about this mysterious, ancient land, and the silent sentinels that still stand today, guarding the secrets from an age long past.
